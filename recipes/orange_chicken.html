Orange Chicken & Broccoli

A delicious and healthy meal of tender chicken
and fresh broccoli.

Who doesn't love a little sweet and sour kinda bowl from time to time?

Try this incredibly tasty dish featuring all the sweet and sour flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 lbs chicken breast - cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons arrowroot flour
  • 3 tablespoons avocado oil
  • 2 cups of broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • salt & pepper for taste
For the sauce:
  • 1/2 cup fresh orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons coconut aminos
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1-inch knob fresh ginger, peeled and minced
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon arrowroot flour
  • 1 tablespoon orange zest
Garnishes:
  • chopped green onions
  • toasted sesame seeds
  • orange zest
  • red pepper flackes (optional)

Steps:

  1. In a bowl coat evenly chicken cubes with salt, peper and 2 tablespoons arrowroot.
  2. Heat avocado oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the coated chicken sear for about 2-3 minutes on each side until starting to
    brown but not fully cooked through.
  4. Transfer to a plate and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet used for the chicken, combine orange juice, coconut aminos,
    seasame oil, garlic, ginger, and rice vinegar.
  6. Stir, bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
  7. Add 1/2 teaspoon arrowroot flour and continue to stir until thickened.
  8. Add the chicken back into the skillet, toss in the sause and stir in orange zest.
  9. Push chicken to one side fo the skillet.
  10. Add broccoli florets and 2 tablespoons of water.
  11. Cover with a lid until the broccoli stems and chicken is cooked through, about 5 minutes.
  12. Once broccoli is tender, season with salt and peper.
  13. Serve warm with garnish.
